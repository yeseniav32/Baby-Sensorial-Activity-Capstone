As a woman who one day wishes to have a family of her own, when I met Gaby, I was blown away with the techniques and activities she was implementing to her children. It dawned on me, I am sure I wouldn't be the only person who can benefit from this kind of information and knowledge. We decided to partner up, and bring to you Activate Baby!

Mission

Inform parents of alternatives of engagement and developement of their children. So, the children can activate and unleash their full potential.

Vision

Provide the the knowledge to not only first time parents, reoccurring, or even provide insight to those who have interest in the early developement of a child.
